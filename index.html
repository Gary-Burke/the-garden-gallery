About Us

What we offer

At the Garden Gallery, we offer an engaging and informative tour of our French-styled garden. Guests are guided through the thoughtfully designed space while receiving in-depth explanations on the techniques and practices used to style and maintain this tranquil haven. The experience is enriched by the exchange of ideas, as visitors often share their own insights and tips for garden care. Following the garden tour, guests are invited to explore our intimate gallery space, which features a curated selection of artwork by local artists. All displayed pieces are available for purchase at affordable prices, providing a wonderful opportunity to support regional talent and take home a unique piece of art.
