About Us

What we offer

At the Garden Gallery, we provide an interactive tour of our french styled garden. We provide detailed and in-depth explanations of how we style and maintain our peace of heaven. During the tour, visitors also often share their tips and tricks regarding styling and maintaining such gardens. After the garden tour, we will move to the little gallery, where we showcase art from local artists. Every visitor will be allowed to purchase an art piece at an affordable price.

Entrance Fees

There is a 5 Euro entrance fee per visitor, but this not only buys you an amazing tour of our gorgeous garden and exquisite gallery, it also provides you with some hot and cold refreshments to enjoy on the day.
